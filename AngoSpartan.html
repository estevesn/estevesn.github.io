About Us


AngoSpartan is an organization of Angolan Students at MSU campus whos seek to share Angola's candos and lifestyle with MSU commnunity through cultural and educational programs, such as dances, poetry, music, workshops, etc.


What We Stand For


Divesrity and Integration: We accept you regardless of race, nationality, gender, sexual orientation, etc..

Education: Know Us, Discover Angola

Joyful Moments: We want you to have the best time in your college years, allow us to be part of it.

Excellence: Join us in a journey to academic excellence and internationalization..
